The Best Movies According Pedro Vilas Boas

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Remember the Titans

At the same time that the film addresses the racial problem of black people's integration into society, it tells a dramatic and engaging story of the resilience of a football team.

Replacements

An unnamed (fictional) professional football league is hit with a players' strike with four games left in the season. Washington Sentinels[3][4] owner Edward O'Neil calls a former coach of his, Jimmy McGinty, telling him that the league's going to finish the regular season with replacement players, and asks McGinty to return to coach the Sentinels the rest of the season, adding that winning three of the last four games will get the Sentinels into the playoffs. McGinty accepts on the condition that he can sign the players he wants without O'Neil's interference.
